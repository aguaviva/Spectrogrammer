<i>Spectrogrammer</i> helps you analyze audio and vibrations, you can spot issues, tune instruments and identify noises byt their footprint.

<br><b>Features:</b>

- Runs on Android and Linux
- Frequency analysis
  - 4096 bands FFT
  - configurable
    - decay
    - averaging
- Visualization
  - Spectrogram
  - Waterfall
  - Uses a magma heatmap
  - When hold is active a binary heatmap is used to shows above/below values
        Linear / Log scales for both axis
  - Hold function to capture refrence levels
  - Save/load/delete/rename for comparing
- High performance, developed entirely in C/C++
- Multiplatform, runs also on linux
